# Evidence Support Gap Packet

## Review Goal

Check whether the packet distinguishes supported claims from claims that need stronger evidence or caveats.

## Claims

| Claim | Evidence label | Caveat |
| --- | --- | --- |
| The review packet identifies one repeatable support gap. | Evidence label A | The label is synthetic shorthand, not proof by itself. |
| The packet's caveats are enough for a downstream action. | Evidence label B | The caller still owns proof thresholds and routing. |
| The packet can be accepted without follow-up. | Evidence label C | The evidence is partial and should stay caveated. |

## Expected Reviewer Posture

- Preserve the distinction between evidence labels and source truth.
- Flag the unsupported acceptance claim as a finding.
- Recommend evidence collection, caveat revision, or follow-up tracking only.
- Keep approval, routing, remediation ownership, publication, retention, and external action caller-owned.
